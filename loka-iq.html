Rules & Regulations
Please read carefully
By confirming your booking and entering the premises, you agree to comply with the following terms and conditions.
1 General Rules, Conduct & Restrictions
  • Guests must behave responsibly and respectfully toward property, staff, and others.
  • Fights, violence, abusive behavior, or inappropriate language will NOT be tolerated and may result in immediate eviction without refund.
  • Use of narcotics, drugs, or illegal substances is strictly prohibited.
  • Loud music or disturbance after 11:00 PM is not allowed unless pre-approved.
  • Staff assistance is available only until 11:00 PM.
  • Only registered guests are allowed; outside visitors require prior approval.
2 Property Use, Cleanliness & Environment
  • Guests must maintain the property in good condition.
  • Any damage to property, furniture, appliances, or surroundings will be charged.
  • Trash must be disposed of in designated bins only; littering is strictly prohibited.
  • Respect nature, plants, animals, and surroundings.
  • Any environmental damage or excessive littering will attract penalties.
3 Safety, Liability & Surveillance
  • Guests stay at the property at their own risk.
  • Management is not responsible for personal injuries, accidents, loss or theft of belongings, or damage/theft of vehicles.
  • Parking is at owner's risk.
  • The property is under CCTV surveillance in common areas. Footage may be used in case of disputes or legal requirements.
4 Pool Rules
  • Pool timings: 7:00 AM to 10:30 PM.
  • Shower before entering is mandatory.
  • Only proper waterproof/swimwear is allowed.
  • Glass items, sharp objects, and food are strictly prohibited near the pool.
  • No unsafe behavior — running, diving in shallow areas, etc.
  • Children must be supervised at all times.
  • Pool usage is at the guest's own risk.
5 Pets & Bonfire
  • Pets are allowed on the property but strictly NOT allowed inside rooms.
  • Pet owners must clean any litter/waste and are responsible for any damage caused.
  • Bonfire is available only on prior request and must be used responsibly.
6 Check-in, Check-out & ID Policy
  • Guests must follow agreed check-in and check-out timings.
  • Original Government-issued ID proof is mandatory at check-in.
  • ID will be returned at checkout upon proper handover of keys.
  • Late check-out may incur additional charges.
7 Penalties, Enforcement & Rights
  • Any rule violation may result in immediate eviction without refund, monetary fines, or legal action.
  • Management reserves the right to refuse entry, cancel booking, or evict guests for non-compliance.

Agreement

By staying at the property, you confirm that you have read and understood all terms and conditions, and agree to abide by all rules stated above.
